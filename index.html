team Tiny House

Mission


Climate change is all around us, yet, in our everyday lives, most of us have done little to shift the course of its rapid progression. Team Tiny House’s goal is to: create an easily constructible tiny house on wheels out of sustainable materials. We want this tiny house to be makeable with just 1-3 people, so it is easy to build for the consumer. We are unique from existing tiny houses because we are (1) a tiny house (2) naturally sourced (3) environmentally friendly (4) easily setup-able for the everyday human.

Team Tiny House is an organization of Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology students who strive to innovate a sustainable tiny house to contribute to our mission of creating a healthy future for our planet. We meet three times a week and have five sub-teams for different tiny house systems. Not only do we engineer a sustainable tiny house, but also volunteer at community events to raise awareness about climate change and promote sustainable living. Our goal is to create sustainable tiny house kits to donate to high schools in impoverished areas.

The Executive committe includes the leaders of each subteam and we work together to keep the team orginized and on track.

Systems

The Systems team is responsible for designing and setting up the water and heating/cooling systems for the tiny house. We take care of plumbing, water collection, waste management, insulation, ventilation, and temperature control.

Structural Design

The Structural Design team drafts the blueprints of the tiny house and makes sure the house meets code.
The Interior Construction team designs and Furnishes the interior of the Tiny House to get the most out of a small space. Our responsibilities include the kitchen, storage cabinets, loft, and the lounge area.
